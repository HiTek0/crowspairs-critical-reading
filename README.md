# crowspairs-critical-reading
This notebook takes a Science and Technology Studies (STS) perspective to critically assess the CrowS-Pairs benchmark by Nangia at al. 
It takes a look at a range of concrete sentence-pairs from the dataset, investigates their shortcomings and develops two lines of critique: 
One that is of methodological nature and one that is of conceptual nature. It then takes these two critiques and reflects upon them to integrate them into a broader, more general critique on the idea of bias-benchmarks.

## Files

- `crowspairs-critical-reading.ipynb` — the notebook itself
- `crows_pairs.csv` — the CrowS-Pairs dataset (downloaded from the authors' official repository)

## Requirements

- Python 3
- pandas
